Car Culture 101

Why Do We Love Cars?

- Filler 1 (Work-in-Progress)
Why do we have such a deep connection with cars? That is a question I find getting asked much too often by people who are not part of car culture. Some people might think they are just buckets with four wheels, but to us cars are much more than that. Its something that you modify to express yourself, that freedom of expression to show who you are in the form of your car.
